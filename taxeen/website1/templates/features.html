Understanding how Nigerian tax works

Progressive Tax Bands

Nigeria uses a progressive tax system where your income is taxed in bands. Each band has a different rate, so you only pay higher rates on the portion of income that falls within that band.

  • First ₦800,000 is tax-free
  • Automated calculation of each band
  • Consolidated Relief Allowance included
  • Pension contributions deducted
